The Drugs You Take

What are drugs?

Drugs are substances that affect an organism's physiology or psychology. Medicine, because it affects certain parts of the body (i.e. blocks neurotransmitters to reduce nausea), is considered a drug. However, drugs generally have side effects, and some side effects are worse than others. It's mainly because of these varying side effects that children are told not to take drugs, even though it includes medications.

Why Do People Take Drugs?

Peer pressure, curiousity, and simple enjoyment of the effects of drugs are all contributing factors. Afterwards, addiction may become a problem. But initially, people continue taking drugs because they enjoy the high it can give them.
